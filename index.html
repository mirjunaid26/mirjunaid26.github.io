I am a Research Engineer at Ecole Centrale Nantes, specializing in High Performance Computing (HPC) and Scientific Computing. My research focuses on developing and optimizing numerical methods for complex engineering simulations.

Previously, I completed my PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Kashmir and my Master's from NIT Srinagar.
